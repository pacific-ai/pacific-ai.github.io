Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some fequently asked questions.

  • How does the MRI to 3D conversion process work?

    The MRI to 3D conversion process involves advanced algorithms and image processing techniques. We analyze the MRI data and extract detailed information to construct a 3D model. Our platform automates this process, making it quick and efficient for healthcare professionals.

  • Is the converted 3D model an accurate representation of the patient's anatomy?

    Yes, the converted 3D model provides an accurate representation of the patient's anatomy based on the MRI data. However, it is important to note that the 3D model is a visual representation and should be used in conjunction with clinical expertise for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

  • How secure is my patient's data during the conversion process?

    We take data security seriously. Your patient's data is stored on client side during the conversion process. We adhere to strict privacy standards and comply with regulatory guidelines to ensure the confidentiality and protection of patient information.

  • What are the benefits of using 3D models in medical diagnostics?

    3D models offer several benefits in medical diagnostics. They provide enhanced visualization and spatial awareness, enabling doctors to identify anomalies, locate specific structures, and assess relationships between anatomical regions. This aids in making more accurate and informed diagnoses, resulting in improved patient care.

  • Can I use the 3D models for surgical planning?

    Yes, the 3D models generated from MRI scans can be utilized for surgical planning. The interactive nature of the models allows surgeons to visualize and assess the patient's anatomy from different angles. This aids in preoperative planning, optimizing surgical approaches, and improving patient outcomes.
